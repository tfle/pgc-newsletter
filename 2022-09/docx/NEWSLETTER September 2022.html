Experience Hong Kong – Saturday 27th August 2022

Thank you for those who could make it to the Experience Hong Kong Celebration on Saturday 27th August 2022.

Hong Kong-Australia Business Association (HKABA) held a special one-off Hong Kong Experience on Saturday 27th August 2022.

 

2022 marks the 25th anniversary of the establishment of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR) with the theme of "A New Era – Stability • Prosperity • Opportunity". 

 

Fun, food and culture were featured throughout the day, with Sydneysiders getting the chance to experience the best of Hong Kong…. without ever setting a foot on a plane! "Experience Hong Kong" was held at the Hordern Pavilion, Moore Park, Sydney. The day was filled with the amazing sights, tastes and sounds of Hong Kong, from the ever-popular lion dance to Cantopop songs, plus a vast array of performances including martial arts, Taichi, Cantonese Opera, live band, children’s performances, and musicians showcasing traditional Chinese instruments.

 

More info on event website: https://experiencehk.com.au/

Burnout Seminar

 

Have you ever felt Burnout? Burnout is a state of exhaustion that stems from excessive and prolonged stress (did anyone say Post-Grad studies?). It occurs when you feel overwhelmed, emotionally drained, and unable to meet constant demands.

 

Bridey Martin, who is an excellent Student Support Advisor at UNSW, will be leading a seminar on the 22nd September at 11am. Here she will inform you of what Burnout is, how to prevent it, and what to do when you find yourself in a state of Burnout.

 

Sign up using the Eventbrite link:
