🇦🇺 Canberra
From 15/12/2021-27/06/2022, there were roughly 400 postgraduate students with one student club, Arc Canberra. Due to the pandemic, many postgraduates intend to work from home, and up to 14 postgrads often attend the activity. With Arc Canberra's effort, there are many more postgrads engaging in the activities. As Canberra Campus Officer of PGC, my strategy is to raise awareness of the presence of PGC and strengthen the relationship between PGC Sydney and PGC Canberra.
